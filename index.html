Why do you need WonderCal?

Trying to schedule a group meeting can feel like herding cats! You’d think everyone having a smartphone and calendar with them all the time would help, but these calendars don’t talk to each other! The result: zillions of emails/texts or tedious, manual tools.

What does it do?

Wondercal eliminates this painful back and forth, by learning when everyone is busy and scheduling around these times! With WonderCal, you know if people are free before you try and schedule the meeting.

How does it work?

Step 1 - Tell WonderCal when and with whom you want to meet. If they’ve never used WonderCal before, we’ll ask them to link their calendars.

Step 2 - WonderCal will use what it knows about the participants’ availability to pick the best times to meet.

Step 3 - Pick the time you like from these options, and WonderCal will send an invitation so no one forgets.
