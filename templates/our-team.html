Our Team

James

James is co-founder and CTO of SmartBod, a startup applying machine learning and intelligence to women’s health. He is an entrepreneur at Foundry@CITRIS, QB3 (California Institute for Quantitative Biosciences), and Free Ventures, and is president of the Berkeley Entrepreneurs Association. James managed product (predictive models, analytics, and financial tools) development at Bridgewater Associates, leading a team of four analysts, and in coordination with sales/client teams helped oversee more than $30 billion in client relationships. He has worked with nonprofits all over the world on technology and operations, as a co-founder and lead consultant at SEEDS Consulting, and is a current board director at WomensTrust, a microfinance organization in Ghana. James is an alumnus of Dartmouth, Berkeley, and Hacker School.
