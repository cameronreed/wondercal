Our Team

James

James is co-founder and CTO of SmartBod, a startup applying machine learning and intelligence to women’s health. He is an entrepreneur at Foundry@CITRIS, QB3 (California Institute for Quantitative Biosciences), and Free Ventures, and is president of the Berkeley Entrepreneurs Association. James managed product (predictive models, analytics, and financial tools) development at Bridgewater Associates, leading a team of four analysts, and in coordination with sales/client teams helped oversee more than $30 billion in client relationships.

Cameron

Cameron is a master’s student at the UC Berkeley School of Information, where she is focusing on user experience design and information visualization. Before starting the Master of Information Management and Systems program at Berkeley, she was a grants management specialist at the National Park Service. Cameron has also worked as a freelance cartographer, and contributed three maps to Food: An Atlas, a crowd-sourced and collaborative publishing project.

Joe

Joe is an MBA candidate at UC Berkeley’s Haas School focused on technology and innovation. Before Berkeley, he was a product manager at Bloomspot (acquired by JPMorgan Chase)—a startup delivering hyper-local, hyper-targeted advertising and loyalty programs for small and medium businesses—and a consultant. At Haas, Joe is co-president of the Haas Technology Club and works closely with the various schools at Berkeley to bring together students of various backgrounds to spark innovation and multidisciplinary collaboration. Most recently, Joe joined LinkedIn as an intern on their Insights strategy team.

Irina

Irina is currently a student at UC Berkeley, pursuing a Master's in Information Management and Systems degree. Right before Berkeley, Irina mostly worked in the finance. She held such positions as a Business Analyst at GoPago Inc which was later acquired by Amazon. Prior to GoPago she worked as a Financial Advisor at Morgan Stanley and as a Financial Analyst at M Financial Group. She is also in the Leadership team at AmBAR (American Association of Russian-Speaking Professionals) located in Palo Alto, CA.

Vivek

Vivek is a seasoned marketing professional who has helped clients, big and small, across three continents to grow their business and stay ahead of the competition. With a strong background in engineering and MarCom, Vivek is proficient at applying science and art to solve the most pressing business problems of Fortune 500 clients. Currently pursuing an MBA from Berkeley-Haas, Vivek is excited about pursuing full-time product marketing roles in the technology and digital media industry.
