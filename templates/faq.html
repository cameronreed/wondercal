Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Who should use WonderCal?

    If you’ve ever wished that you could make scheduling group meetings WAY easier, WonderCal is for you. We’ve had people from students to freelancers to socialites tell us that they wanted a better way to schedule meetings for contacts scattered across Google Calendar, iCal, Outlook… WonderCal solves this by syncing between calendars and letting you schedule meetings in three clicks.

  2. How does WonderCal know what the best time is?

    Once you invite your contacts to a meeting, we find times that work for everyone’s calendar, making it easy for you to see when everyone can meet.

  3. How does WonderCal synchronize my calendars?

    After you give us permission through sign-up, we handle communicating with your calendaring service. This ensures that you always have an up-to-date view of everyone’s availability!

  4. Who can view my calendar?

    No one but you. Others can see when everyone in the meeting is available, but cannot view your calendar.

  5. Is WonderCal safe/private?

    WonderCal does not store your passwords, and uses industry best-practice protocols (OAuth) to securely connect to your calendaring services. Any data that we temporarily store to help make your experience smoother is encrypted and held on our secure servers.

  6. How do I use WonderCal as my primary calendar?

    Right now, we are focused on making the experience of scheduling awesome (or at least painless), but check back with us soon!
